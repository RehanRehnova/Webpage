How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by complexity. A landing page takes 1–2 weeks, a full web app 6–12 weeks, and enterprise systems 3–6 months. We provide accurate timelines after our free discovery call.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. We work with clients across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific. Our team operates across time zones to ensure responsive communication wherever you are.
What AI frameworks and models do you use?
We're model-agnostic — OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and open-source models like Llama. For agentic systems we use LangChain, AutoGen, and CrewAI depending on the use case.
Who owns the code and IP after delivery?
You do — 100%. We sign NDAs before any project discussion. All deliverables, including source code and design files, transfer to you upon final payment. We retain no rights whatsoever.
What does post-launch support include?
Bug fixes, security patches, performance monitoring, minor feature updates, and 24/7 uptime monitoring. Support duration ranges from 30 days (Starter) to 12 months (Enterprise).
Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Yes — integrations are a core specialty. We regularly connect with Salesforce, HubSpot, SAP, QuickBooks, Stripe, Twilio, and virtually any platform with an API or standard protocol.
