FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers about our UI/UX & Branding service. Still have questions?

Ask Us Directly
Do you design mobile and desktop?

Yes. Every UI design project covers mobile, tablet, and desktop breakpoints as standard. We design mobile-first and adapt upward, ensuring the experience is exceptional at every screen size.

Can you redesign an existing product?

Absolutely. Redesigns are a significant part of our work. We start with a thorough heuristic audit of your existing product, identifying every usability issue and opportunity before we draw a single new screen.

What format are the design deliverables?

All designs are delivered in Figma with organised pages, named layers, and auto-layout components. Assets are exported in SVG, PNG, and WebP. Brand work is additionally delivered in PDF and source AI/EPS formats.

How do revisions work?

We present designs at key milestones and collect all feedback in one round before implementing changes. Growth and Enterprise packages include unlimited revisions. Brand Identity includes 3 revision rounds.

Can you work with our existing developers?

Yes. We regularly deliver designs to in-house or third-party dev teams. We provide a thorough handoff session, annotated Figma specs, and remain available for design QA questions throughout development.

Do I own all the design files?

100%. All Figma source files, brand assets, exported icons, and documentation transfer to you on final payment. We sign NDAs before any project discussion and retain zero IP rights.
