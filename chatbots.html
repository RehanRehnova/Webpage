FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers about our AI Chatbot service. Still have questions?

Ask Us Directly
How accurate will the chatbot be?

RAG-based chatbots trained on your actual documentation typically achieve 90–95% accuracy on queries within their knowledge domain. We run accuracy benchmarking and share metrics before launch.

Can it handle complex multi-turn conversations?

Yes. We build full dialogue management with memory — the bot tracks context across a conversation, handles follow-up questions, and resolves references correctly across multiple turns.

What happens when the bot doesn't know an answer?

We configure graceful fallback responses and smart escalation triggers. Unresolved queries are routed to human agents with full conversation history — no frustrating restarts for your users.

How long does deployment take?

A basic FAQ chatbot can be live in 2–3 weeks. A full multi-channel AI assistant with CRM integration typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on knowledge base size and integration complexity.

Can I update the knowledge base myself?

Yes. We build an admin panel where your team can add, edit, and remove content, set new FAQ entries, and see which queries the bot is struggling with — no developer required.

Do I own the source code?

100%. All source code, prompt configurations, and documentation transfer to you on final payment. We sign NDAs before any project discussion and retain zero IP rights.
